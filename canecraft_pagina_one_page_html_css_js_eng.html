Our story

Distilled from Dominican Sugar Cane.

CaneCraft, a groundbreaking gluten‑free vodka crafted from sugar cane from the Caribbean island of the Dominican Republic. Utilizing sustainable methods, we blend the finest all‑natural sugar‑free ingredients to create a truly sophisticated experience.

Our journey begins in the fertile lands of San Pedro de Macorís Province, renowned for its untouched soil and abundant resources. The pristine rivers flowing through the region nourish our cane plants for an impressive 12–14 months, ensuring unparalleled quality.

With CaneCraft, you will indulge in a rich, harmonious flavor profile that boasts viscosity and an exquisitely smooth finish. Join us as we redefine elegance in every cocktail.

Origin and process

Born in San Pedro de Macorís, D.R. Our cane is nourished by pristine river waters and harvested at peak maturity. The mash is fermented at low temperature to preserve aromatic purity, then distilled four times to achieve a silky texture.
